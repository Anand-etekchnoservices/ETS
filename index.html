RESPONSIVE DESIGN

In responsive design, the device does the work and automatically adjusts according to a device’s screen size (large or small) and orientation (landscape or portrait). It switches between these options on-the-fly.

A responsive site means no more pinching and zooming, and no more side scrolling, to see an entire site that doesn't fit on a mobile screen. And It will be better user experience reduces bounce rates, boosts website conversions and improves brand perception.
